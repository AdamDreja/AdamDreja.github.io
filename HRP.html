About The Project

Home ERP is the first-of-its-kind Enterprise Resource Planning system designed specifically for managing households. Its comprehensive features include:

  • Task Management: Organize and assign daily household tasks.
  • Finance Management: Track expenses and budget effectively.
  • Reminders: Set and manage important dates and alerts.
  • Contact and People Management: Keep track of family and household contacts.
This ERP streamlines day-to-day activities, offering families an innovative way to stay organized and efficient.
