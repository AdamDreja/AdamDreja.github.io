About The Project

Tasheel is the first web-based Point of Sale (POS) system in Libya, offering cutting-edge features for businesses of all sizes. The system provides tools for:

  • Calculating daily, weekly, and monthly income with ease.
  • Adding, editing, and removing products and inventory seamlessly.
  • Tracking purchases and associating sales with customers.
  • Offering a fully customizable layout with unique views for each company.
  • Providing multi-user access with differentiated privileges for admins and users.
  • Allowing super admin management for overseeing company accounts and their associations.
Tasheel is designed to adapt to your business needs, ensuring flexibility and efficiency in managing your operations.
