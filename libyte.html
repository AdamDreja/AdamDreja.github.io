About The Project

Libyte is a tailored ERP system developed for Mass, a leading engineering company based in Libya. Designed to address the specific needs of the client, Libyte includes:

  • Comprehensive financial management, including income and profit tracking.
  • Advanced analytics and reporting for strategic decision-making.
  • Shipment and truck tracking to streamline logistics.
  • Purchases and sales management to optimize operations.
  • Customer database management for enhanced client relationship handling.
  • Fully customized features to align with Mass's operational workflows.
Libyte empowers Mass to manage its operations efficiently, with an intuitive interface and robust backend to support growth and innovation.
